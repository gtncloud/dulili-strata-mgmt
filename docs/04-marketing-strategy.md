# StrataHub — Marketing Strategy

## 1. Brand Identity

### Brand Positioning
**"The modern way to manage your building."**

StrataHub positions itself as the **Canva of strata management** — making something traditionally complex, ugly, and frustrating into something beautiful, simple, and even enjoyable.

### Brand Values
- **Transparency** — Open financials, clear communication
- **Simplicity** — Complex tasks made effortless
- **Community** — Bringing neighbours together
- **Trust** — Bank-level security, Australian-hosted data

### Brand Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Plain English (avoid strata jargon where possible)
- Helpful and empowering
- Australian-flavoured (not American corporate speak)

### Visual Identity
- **Primary colour**: Deep Teal (#0D9488) — trust, sophistication
- **Secondary colour**: Warm Amber (#F59E0B) — energy, warmth, community
- **Accent**: Soft White (#F8FAFC) — clean, modern
- **Typography**: Inter (headings) + DM Sans (body)
- **Logo concept**: Minimalist building icon with connected nodes (community + structure)

## 2. Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Grassroots Launch (Months 1-3)

**Target**: Self-managed strata schemes in Sydney (inner city apartments, 6-20 lots)

| Channel | Action | Budget |
|---|---|---|
| **Direct outreach** | Door-knock 50 apartment buildings in inner Sydney | $0 |
| **Strata committee LinkedIn** | Connect with committee chairs, message personally | $0 |
| **Local Facebook Groups** | Join Sydney apartment/strata groups, provide value, share product | $0 |
| **Product Hunt launch** | Launch on Product Hunt for tech-savvy early adopters | $0 |
| **Free tier** | Generous free plan to drive adoption, then upsell | $0 |
| **Referral program** | First 3 months free for referred buildings | $0 |

**Goal**: 20 buildings onboarded, 5 paying customers

### Phase 2: Content & SEO Engine (Months 3-6)

Build an organic inbound engine targeting strata-related search queries.

| Content Type | Topics | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **Blog posts** | "How to Read a Strata Report", "NSW Strata Law Changes 2026", "Strata Levy Calculator" | 2x/week |
| **Guides** | "Ultimate Guide to Self-Managing Your Strata", "First-Time Apartment Buyer's Strata Checklist" | 1x/month |
| **Calculators/Tools** | Free strata levy calculator, maintenance cost estimator | 2 tools |
| **YouTube** | "Strata Explained" video series (3-5 min explainers) | 1x/week |
| **Email newsletter** | "The Strata Digest" — weekly AU strata news & tips | Weekly |

**SEO Target Keywords**:
| Keyword | Monthly Searches (AU) | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|
| strata management | 8,100 | High |
| strata fees | 4,400 | Medium |
| strata report | 3,600 | Medium |
| strata levies | 2,900 | Medium |
| self managed strata | 1,600 | Low |
| strata committee responsibilities | 1,300 | Low |
| strata maintenance request | 720 | Low |
| strata management software | 590 | Medium |

### Phase 3: Partnership & B2B Sales (Months 6-12)

Target professional strata management firms.

| Channel | Action | Budget |
|---|---|---|
| **Strata industry events** | Sponsor/exhibit at SCA (Strata Community Association) conferences | $5,000-10,000 |
| **SCA partnerships** | Become SCA technology partner | $2,000 membership |
| **Strata manager webinars** | Host "Digital Transformation in Strata" webinars | $500/event |
| **Channel partnerships** | Partner with strata lawyers, valuers, insurance brokers for referrals | Commission-based |
| **Case studies** | Publish ROI-driven case studies from early adopters | $0 |
| **LinkedIn Ads** | Targeted ads to strata managers and property professionals | $2,000/month |
| **Google Ads** | Bid on "strata management software" keywords | $1,500/month |

## 3. Customer Acquisition Funnels

### Funnel 1: Self-Managed Strata (B2C)

```
Awareness      →  Google search / Facebook group / word of mouth
  ↓
Interest       →  Landing page + free tools (levy calculator)
  ↓
Sign Up        →  Free plan (up to 6 lots)
  ↓
Activation     →  Wizard helps set up building, invite residents
  ↓
Engagement     →  Use noticeboard, submit maintenance request
  ↓
Conversion     →  Hit limits on free plan → upgrade to Community ($29/m)
  ↓
Referral       →  "Invite another building" → 1 month free
```

### Funnel 2: Strata Managers (B2B)

```
Awareness      →  SCA events / LinkedIn / Google Ads / webinars
  ↓
Interest       →  Demo request on website
  ↓
Qualification  →  Discovery call: firm size, current software, pain points
  ↓
Demo           →  45-min personalised demo with their data
  ↓
Trial          →  14-day free trial on 3 buildings
  ↓
Conversion     →  Professional plan @ $99/building/month
  ↓
Expansion      →  Roll out across all managed buildings
```

## 4. Retention & Growth Strategy

### Reducing Churn
| Strategy | Details |
|---|---|
| **Onboarding excellence** | Guided setup wizard, video walkthroughs, personal onboarding call for B2B |
| **Weekly engagement email** | "Your building this week" summary with key updates |
| **Feature education** | In-app tooltips, monthly "Tips & Tricks" webinar |
| **Community building** | StrataHub Community Forum for managers to share best practices |
| **Feedback loop** | In-app NPS surveys, feature request board (Canny-style) |

### Expansion Revenue
| Strategy | Details |
|---|---|
| **Upsell premium features** | AI document search, advanced analytics, contractor marketplace |
| **Cross-sell add-ons** | Strata certificate generation, custom reporting |
| **Seat expansion** | More buildings = more revenue (usage-based pricing) |
| **Annual plans** | 20% discount for annual pre-payment (improves cash flow) |
