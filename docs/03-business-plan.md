## 2. Market Opportunity

### Australian Strata Market Size

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total strata schemes in AU | ~350,000 |
| Total strata lots (units) | ~3.2 million |
| Annual strata levies collected | ~$12 billion |
| Strata management industry revenue | ~$7 billion |
| Annual growth rate | ~4.5% |
| % of Australians in strata | ~10% (and growing) |

### Market Trends Driving Growth
1. **Urbanisation** — 75% of new housing in Sydney/Melbourne is apartments
2. **Regulatory complexity** — New strata laws in NSW (2016), VIC (2021) demand better tools
3. **Digital transformation** — Post-COVID acceleration of digital adoption in property
4. **Transparency demands** — Owners increasingly demand financial visibility
5. **Self-management rise** — More small schemes going self-managed to save on fees

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

| Segment | # of Schemes | Avg Revenue/Scheme/Year | Total Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Professionally managed (large) | 50,000 | $1,200 | $60M |
| Professionally managed (small) | 100,000 | $600 | $60M |
| Self-managed | 200,000 | $300 | $60M |
| **Total SAM** | **350,000** | | **$180M/year** |

We target capturing **1% of SAM in Year 1** ($1.8M ARR) and **5% by Year 3** ($9M ARR).

## 3. Revenue Model

### Pricing Strategy: Tiered SaaS Subscription

#### For Strata Managers (B2B)

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $49/month per building | Up to 20 lots, basic modules |
| **Professional** | $99/month per building | Up to 100 lots, all modules, integrations |
| **Enterprise** | $199/month per building | Unlimited lots, white-label, API access, priority support |
| **Volume Discount** | 20% off for 50+ buildings | Applied at Enterprise tier |

#### For Self-Managed Strata (B2C)

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0/month | Up to 6 lots, basic features (noticeboard, documents, directory) |
| **Community** | $29/month per building | Up to 20 lots, maintenance + communication |
| **Community Pro** | $59/month per building | All features including financials + voting |

#### Additional Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description | Revenue Model |
|---|---|---|
| **Transaction Fees** | Online levy payments via Stripe | 1.5% processing fee pass-through |
| **Contractor Marketplace** | Verified tradies bid on maintenance jobs | 5% commission on accepted quotes |
| **Premium Add-ons** | AI document search, advanced analytics, custom reports | $19-49/month |
| **Section 184 Certificates** | Auto-generate strata info certificates | $30 per certificate |
| **Onboarding Services** | White-glove data migration for large firms | $500-2,000 one-time |
| **API Access** | Third-party integrations for accountants, lawyers | $99/month |

## 5. Funding Strategy

### Phase 1: Bootstrap (Months 1-6)
- Self-funded development
- Target: $0-50K personal investment
- Goal: Launch MVP, onboard 20 paying buildings

### Phase 2: Pre-Seed (Month 6-9)
- Raise $200-500K from angel investors
- Target investors: PropTech angels, Australian angel networks (Sydney Angels, Scale Investors)
- Use of funds: First hire, marketing, compliance

### Phase 3: Seed Round (Month 12-18)
- Raise $1-2M from VC
- Target firms: Blackbird Ventures, Square Peg, Airtree, Right Click Capital
- Use of funds: Team expansion, interstate launch, mobile apps

### Investor Pitch Points
1. **$180M SAM** in Australia alone, growing 4.5%/year
2. **Recurring SaaS revenue** with low churn (strata schemes are sticky)
3. **Regulatory moat** — state-specific compliance is hard to replicate
4. **Expansion path** — NZ, Singapore, Hong Kong have similar strata systems
5. **Strong unit economics** — LTV:CAC ratio target of 5:1
